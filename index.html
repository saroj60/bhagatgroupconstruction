Company Overview

Bhagat Group of Construction Pvt. Ltd.

Bhagat Group of Construction Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated on Bhadra 06, 2075 B.S. under the Private Firm Registration Act 2018. Since its establishment, the company has been engaged in a wide range of civil engineering projects across Nepal, steadily growing its capacity, manpower, and technical expertise.

The company has developed a reputation for delivering quality work with proper planning, coordination, and timely execution. Our major areas of work include building construction, road and highway works, irrigation and drainage facilities, land development, water supply structures, and retrofitting of existing buildings.
